Building a Championship Business

Description

Checklist of what you need to build championship winning businesses according to Alex Hormozi

Components

  • A starving Crowd
  • Grand Slam Offer
  • Persuasion SKills
  • Value
  • Sales
  • Strategies
  • Execute

Steps

  1. identify the starving crowd with massive pain, purchasing power, that are target-able & growing
  2. create an offer so good people feel stupid saying no (tremendous value at a premium price)
  3. a grand slam offer addresses: Dream Outcome, Problems/Obstacles, Lists all Problems as SOlutions, Maximize Value & profit, Financial High Value High Profit Offer Static
  4. enhance the offer with Scarcity, Urgency, Bonuses, Guarantees and Naming
  5. bundle many offers together, that solve a particular pain & break beliefs that held your customers back in the past - be specific in copy
  6. Value is the Product of Dream Outcome and Perceived Likelihood of Achievement divided by Effort, Sacrifice times Time Delay
  7. decrease the TIme Delay as much as possible, at best give first results as early as possible
  8. C.L.O.S.E.R: Clarify why they are here, Label them with a problem, Overview their past experiences, Sell the Vacation, Explain what is holding them back, Reinforce their decision
  9. to achieve all the above use divergent thinking and inversion thinking models
  10. the best way to learn is to execute. go out there and get your first 100k already
