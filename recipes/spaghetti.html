Spaghetti

Description

Italian dish of long & thin noodles usually eaten with bolognese sauce

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomatoes & Vegetables
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Mince, or similar
  • Herbs & Spices
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. cook the noodles in boiling and slightly salted water
  2. cut the tomatoes and other vegetables
  3. roast onions, mince (or similar) and vegetables in a pan
  4. add tomato sauce to the pan
  5. serve the dish warm with shredded cheese
