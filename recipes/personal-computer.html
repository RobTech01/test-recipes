Computer Build

Description

Personal Computer re-imagined as simple & efficient as possible

Components

  • CPU & GPU, or APU
  • RAM, Storage
  • Motherboard
  • Power Supply
  • Monitor, Peripherals
  • Fans & Cooling
  • Cables
  • Cable Organizer (Zip Ties & similar)
  • Operating System

Steps

  1. install RAM, CPU/APU and if applicable GPU & Storage on Motherboard
  2. connect the Cables from the Power supply to the Motherboard (& GPU)
  3. jump-Start the System by shorting the corresponding power pins on the Motherboard with a screwdriver
  4. if the fans are spinning: good Job, else check your cables
  5. turn off the system and plug it in a Monitor and Peripherals
  6. power up the System and follow the specific startup instructions for the Operating System of your choice
